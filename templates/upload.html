Recording Guidelines

📹 Setup

  • Record in landscape mode (horizontal)
  • Laptop on a flat surface at desk level
  • Face and upper body centered in frame
  • Keep 1.5–2 feet from the camera
  • One person in frame only

💡 Lighting

  • Record in a well-lit room
  • Natural light in front of you is best
  • Don't sit with a window behind you
  • Avoid dim or yellow overhead lighting

🎤 Audio

  • Record in a quiet room
  • Speak clearly at a natural pace
  • Make sure your mic is not muted

👤 Appearance

  • Avoid glasses if possible — reflections affect eye contact detection
  • Keep hair away from ears — affects posture scoring

📁 Video Requirements

  • Format: MP4 preferred
  • Length: 30 seconds min, 10 minutes max
  • Resolution: 720p or above

🎯 During Recording

  • Look directly at the camera — this is measured
  • Sit upright — slouching is detected
  • Speak at 120–150 words per minute
  • Avoid: "um", "uh", "like", "actually", "basically"
  • Use natural hand gestures
  • Don't read from a script

❌ Avoid

  • Dark rooms
  • Camera pointing up at you
  • Sitting too far or too close
  • Background noise or music
  • Closing the page before upload completes
